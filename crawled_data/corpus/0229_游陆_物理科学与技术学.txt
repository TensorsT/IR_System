研究领域:We are a research group focusing on experimental studies of ferroic materials and related phenomena. Particularly, we are interested in exotic physics in van der Waals ferroic crystals
，
including but not limited to ferroelectric, ferromagnetic, ferroelastic, multiferroic order parameters and their coupling effects with various energy fields (mechanical, light, electric, magnetic, thermal, etc.).
本课题组主要从事铁性有序材料和相关物理现象的实验研究工作。主要的研究兴趣集中在范德华铁性晶体的奇异物理性质上，包括铁电性、铁磁性、铁弹性、多铁性以及这些序参量和不同能量场（力、光、电、磁、热等）的耦合效应。
