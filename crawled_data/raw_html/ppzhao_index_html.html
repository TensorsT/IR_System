Biography

Biography：

Dr. Pengpeng Zhao is a professor at Soochow University and a leading talent in Suzhou Industrial Park. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Soochow University in 2008. From 2016 to 2017, he was a visiting scholar, working with Professor Hui Xiong at the Data Mining and Business Analysis Laboratory at Rutgers University. In recent years, his research has been supported by Natural Science Foundation of China and Natural Science Foundation of Jiangsu Province. He won two Suzhou Science and Technology Progress Awards. He has published more than 100 papers in international conferences and journals, and 70+ papers were published in CCF rank A/B conferences, such as TKDE, AAAI, IJCAI, ACM MM, WWW, ICDM, CIKM, DASFAA, and ICME. He submitted more than 50 national invention patents applications, of which 50+ are authorized. He has served as editorial board member of the International Journal of Intelligent Information Systems, reviewer of many important academic Journals such as IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering, ACM Transactions on Knowledge Discovery from Data, IEEE Transactions on Big Data, and IEEE Transactions on Cybernetics, as well as program committee member of international conferences such as AAAI, IJCAI, WSDM, CIKM, DASFAA and PAKDD. His current research interests include data mining, deep learning, big data analysis, and recommender systems.
